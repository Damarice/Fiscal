Who We Are

The Centre for Fiscal Affairs (CFA)

Is an independent research and policy advocacy think tank dedicated to promoting fiscal transparency, accountability, and participatory governance. Our mission is to contribute to the creation of a just, fair, and robust society. Since our inception, CFA has established itself as a collaborative force, working alongside like-minded non-state actors, government agencies, and development partners. Our efforts are focused on enhancing fiscal transparency, ensuring accountability, and promoting the prudent use of public resources at both national and sub-national levels. We are committed to empowering citizens to advocate for progressive fiscal systems and processes. Our goal is to ensure that government policies on taxation and spending are equitable and beneficial to the majority, rather than favoring a select few.

Our Vision

A think tank dedicated to fostering a just, fair, and strong society through comprehensive policy research and strategic advocacy.

Our Mission

To promote a people-centered governance system that ensures transparency and accountability in the mobilization and utilization of public resources.

Our Core Values

  • Fairness
  • Accountability
  • Integrity
  • Transparency
