Fiscal Transparency

Effective Governance

The Centre for Fiscal Affairs (CFA) is dedicated to promoting fiscal transparency, accountability, and participatory governance. Our mission is to create a fair and robust society through strategic advocacy and comprehensive policy research. We work collaboratively with government agencies, non-state actors, and development partners to ensure equitable management of public resources.

Advocating for transparent fiscal policies and public spending.

Empowering citizens to participate in governance.

What We Do

Empowering Civic Engagement and Promoting Accountability

CFA employs evidence-based policy research and analysis, strategic communications, and effective advocacy to facilitate civic engagement, inform public discourse on taxation, public debt, and public spending, and promote effective service delivery at sub-national, national, and regional levels. Our work is organized into three key thematic areas, each designed to address critical issues and drive meaningful change.

Our Projects

How We Drive Change Through Our Projects

Our projects are designed to address critical issues through comprehensive research, effective advocacy, and capacity strengthening. Here's an overview of how we work:

  • Research and Policy Analysis
  • Advocacy
  • Capacity Strengthening

Research and Policy Analysis

CFA undertakes evidence-based research to generate insights for informing and influencing policy and advocacy interventions. We produce well-researched, empirical, and accurate policy briefs, reports, and working papers on fiscal transparency, accountability, and prudent resource utilization at national and sub-national levels. Our policy analysis focuses on understanding different policy directions and their effects on citizen well-being, using both qualitative and quantitative data.

Read More

Advocacy

CFA promotes citizen advocacy in the formulation, review, implementation, and oversight of public policies on taxation, public debt, and public spending. We leverage research to support our advocacy efforts, aiming to empower citizens and enhance their capacity to hold governments accountable. CFA also participates in various advocacy campaigns both locally and regionally to strengthen fiscal transparency and accountability for better service delivery.

Read More

Capacity Strengthening

Through interaction with stakeholders and partners, CFA addresses identified gaps by offering tailor-made training that responds to their needs. Our capacity strengthening initiatives are designed to build the skills and knowledge necessary for effective advocacy and implementation of public policies.

Our Impact

Making a Difference with Every Project

At Fiscal Hub, we are committed to driving positive change through our impactful projects. Our efforts extend beyond financial management, as we strive to create sustainable growth and improve the lives of individuals and communities.

Boosting Economic Development

Our strategic financial solutions and advisory services have played a crucial role in supporting local businesses and startups. By providing expert guidance and resources, we help drive economic growth and create job opportunities.

Enhancing Financial Literacy

We are dedicated to improving financial literacy across communities. Our educational programs and workshops empower individuals with the knowledge and skills to make informed financial decisions and manage their resources effectively.

Supporting Sustainable Initiatives

Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our support for eco-friendly projects and initiatives. We work with partners to promote sustainable practices and reduce environmental impact through responsible financial management.

Empowering Local Communities

Through community-focused projects and financial support, we are empowering local groups and organizations to achieve their goals. Our efforts foster community development, promote social welfare, and enhance overall quality of life.
